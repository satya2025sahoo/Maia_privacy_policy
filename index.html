Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 19, 2026

Introduction

This privacy policy governs your use of the "Maia" mobile application (the "App") created by Satya Ranjan Sahoo. This policy outlines the types of data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used, processed, and stored.

Your privacy is critically important to us. All AI inference and conversation processing occurs entirely on your device. No conversation text, AI responses, or personal memory data is ever recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server by Maia's core features.

Data Collection and Use (Permissions)

The App requests the following permissions to provide its features. All data processed via these permissions either stays on your device or is handled by a clearly disclosed third-party service (Google AdMob).

  • Internet Permission: This permission is required for AI model downloads directly to your device's local storage and by Google AdMob to serve advertisements.
  • Foreground Service Permission: Required to run the AI model download as a reliable background task.
  • Post Notifications Permission: Required on Android 13+ to display download progress notifications.
  • Wakelock Permission: Used to keep the CPU active during AI model inference to ensure performance.

On-Device AI Processing

The core feature of Maia is a large language model (LLM) that runs entirely on your device. This means:

  • All conversation messages are processed locally.
  • No prompts or AI outputs are sent to any external server.
  • Personal memories are stored only in a local database on your device.

Because all AI processing is on-device, Satya Ranjan Sahoo has no ability to view or collect any of your conversations.

Local Data Storage (On-Device)

The App stores data locally using SharedPreferences for settings and a Local SQLite Database for chat history and memories. This data is never shared and is deleted if you uninstall the App.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used is Google AdMob. Google may collect device identifiers to serve ads. We do not share your private chat data with Google.

Review Google's privacy policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy
